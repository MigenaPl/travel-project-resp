Why London?

London, the city of dreams

London is a major global city, steeped in history, has been a cultural, economic and political center for centuries. Every turn reveals a different secret and a different story from long ago making the capital a popular tourist destination and known for its historical landmarks, museums, and cultural diversity.

Additionally, London is home to many reputable universities, businesses and cultural institutions, which attract people from all over the world who come to pursue their career or study goals. This may be one of the reasons why London is considered as the city of dreams.

COCKTAIL BARS

My favourite Cocktail Bars in London

Ku Lounge

A Chic Boutique Cocktail Bar, within the streets of Chinatown next to Leicester Square. Voted 'BEST NEW VENUE' by the public through Design My Night.

Address:

1 Newport Pl, London WC2H 7JR

What I like about it

This is one of the best cocktail spots in London. The attention to detail put into the cocktails is simply spot-on and the flavour combinations will leave a long-lasting impression.

Learn more

The Vault

Step through the bookcase at Milroy's of Soho and enter a subterranean world of candlelight and cocktails. The Vault is an award-winning haunt in the cellars underneath Greek Street where the drinks are original, unpretentious, and, most importantly, delicious.

Address:

3 Greek St, London W1D 4NX

What I like about it

A place to discover! Not always easy to get a table but it's such a cozy hidden place with great ambience, the cocktails are simply amazing.

Learn more

Opium Cocktail Bar & Dim Sum Parlour

In the heart of Chinatown, Soho, behind the hidden jade door and up the winding staircase, the award winning 1920's Shanghai themed cocktail bar offers an eclectic range of experimental & creative cocktails, with traditional dim sum served throughout the venue.

Address:

15-16 Gerrard St, London W1D 6JE

What I like about it

It's hidden! And it has an intriguing dimmed candle-lit ambience, perfect for a quiet drink in a more intimate setting.
